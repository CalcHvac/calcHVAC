About

Welcome to Bengal Instruments & Chemicals, LLC (BIC), your trusted bridge between global innovation and Bangladesh’s scientific advancement. We specialize in supplying premium lab equipment—from quality testing solutions to specialty chemicals—sourced directly from trusted USA/European manufacturers. Committed to quality and affordability, we empower industries with world-class equipment while ensuring unmatched service. At BIC, excellence isn’t just delivered—it’s sustained through partnerships built on reliability, precision, and dedicated customer care. Together, let’s elevate Bangladeshi industries to global standards.

Why US?

  • Our Mission
  • Our Vision

At BIC, we bridge global innovation with local impact. Our mission is to empower industries in Bangladesh with world-class laboratory equipment, precision instruments, and specialty chemicals sourced from trusted manufacturers in the USA and Europe—all delivered at competitive pricing. We believe access to superior-quality tools and solutions shouldn’t come at a premium, which is why we combine rigorous quality assurance with cost-effective offerings tailored to meet the evolving needs of water testing, food safety, and beyond. Beyond supply, we pride ourselves on unmatched post-sale support, ensuring seamless operation and lasting value for every product we deliver.

As we grow, we strive to become a registered partner for institutions and a trusted foreign principal for local businesses, fostering long-term collaborations that elevate Bangladesh’s scientific and industrial capabilities. Together, we’re not just delivering equipment—we’re enabling progress, reliability, and excellence, one lab bench at a time.

At BIC, we envision a future where cutting-edge science and industry in Bangladesh thrive with access to the world’s finest tools and solutions. Our mission is to bridge global innovation with local progress by delivering premium-quality laboratory equipment, specialty chemicals, and unmatched post-sale support from trusted U.S. and European partners to laboratories, industries, and researchers across Bangladesh. We believe excellence should be both attainable and sustainable—offering world-class products at competitive prices while prioritizing reliability, responsiveness, and lasting relationships. By empowering our customers with advanced technologies and dedicated service, we strive to fuel breakthroughs in water testing, food safety, and beyond, fostering a culture of precision, trust, and growth. Together, we’re not just supplying tools—we’re building a foundation for a smarter, healthier tomorrow.
